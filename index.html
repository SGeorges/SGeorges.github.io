About

Hello World! I'm Stefano,

I'm an aspiring web developer looking to make a start from unorthodox beginnings. After highschool I attended Muhlenberg College to find what I wanted to do with my life. I had an affinity towards math and sciences but I took this opportunity to take other courses I normally wouldn't have. Even though this time should've been spent discovering I was young and close-minded.

After two years I transferred into Drexel University to pursue a degree in Chemical Engineering. After graduating from Drexel I spent the next five years working in the Environmental Engineering field. With clients ranging from simple family farmers to the US Navy I'd viewed a wide spectrum fo the field I was in, but looking into the future it wasn't where I wanted to be.

It was a field where I felt my creativity and freedom were restricted so I decided to go for broke and change my career pathway to something I personally enjoy. Even though I can't stress how much I loved working outside in the extreme heat or cold, fighting off ticks, chiggers, bees, spiders, and sacrificing my well-being for the sake of the client, it was time for a change.

So here I am. Re-educated and revitalized with the desire to change the world in a vastly different setting. Thanks for checking out my profile!
